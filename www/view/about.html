Cloud Photo Gallery is based on parse api.

Functionality:
- Register as free member.
- Upload Photo after login.
- Photo will be private to your user account.
- You can delete photo any time.
- App user can't see other member's photo.

- All photos are stored on parse cloud.
- No public access to photos other than this app.

Note:
- This application is for parse demo purpose.
- Do not upload photo for storge purpose.
- We may delete photo from parse admin if storage full.
- You can add maximum 24 photos, thereafter add photo button (+) disappear.
